Meet your soap makers, Rachel & Sina.

We like making things, and back in October we decided to make soap.

Why soap?

Well, our work and many other projects we're involved with are digital, so it's really nice to balance that with something hands on. It's been fun to mix it up and make something tangible and sharable.

So hey, friends and family—happy holidays! We've really enjoyed making Paper Boat Soap and thinking of you all in the process.
